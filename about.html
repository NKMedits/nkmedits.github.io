About Me

With over five years of experience as an Editor and Assistant Editor, I’ve contributed to campaigns for Marvel, Disney, Paramount, HBO Max, and Apple TV.

Known for crafting clean, efficient workflows and supporting teams from dailies through delivery, I bring structure to fast-paced editorial environments. Whether cutting short-form narratives or assisting on high-profile marketing campaigns, I approach each project with precision, narrative instinct, and a deep respect for the story.

Collaboration, clarity, and a shared commitment to the creative are at the core of my work. Let’s build something magnetic.
